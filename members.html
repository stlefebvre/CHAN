Members

Many of the FQHC sites are located in officially designated medially under-served areas and/or health professional shortage areas. Currently, the HCCN coverage encompasses four counties in southern New Hampshire representing more than half of the stateâ€™s population, and five centers in Texas.

Membership is available at two levels: Full Membership and Affiliate Membership

Full Membership is open to non-profit primary health care agencies and affords access to all HCCN services and resources. Full Members have representation on the Board of Directors.

Affiliate members do not participate on the Board of Directors. Some Affiliate members house their own IT infrastructure and others access CHAN systems.
